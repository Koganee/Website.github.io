About Roderick Barker

Roderick Barker is a 20 year old author from Ireland. In his free time, he enjoys travelling, reading, and writing. His favourite book genres are fantasy and horror.

Sometimes he watches movies too, and finds anything with good music and well-done cinematography enjoyable. Oppenheimer is his favourite movie that has come out recently.
